Revolutionizing Nutrition with Millets

The Shree Anna initiative represents India's commitment to promoting millets as nutritional powerhouses for the 21st century. By rebranding millets as "Shree Anna" (the respected food), we're driving a nutritional revolution that benefits farmers, consumers, and the environment.
