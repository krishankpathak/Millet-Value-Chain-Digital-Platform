How Direct Trade Works

1. Farmer Registration & Product Listing

Farmers register on the platform and list their millet produce with quality specifications, quantity available, and expected price range.

2. Buyer Discovery & Matching

Our AI-powered system matches farmers with suitable buyers based on location, product requirements, and trade history.

3. Transparent Bidding & Negotiation

Buyers can place bids or make offers, and farmers can accept, reject, or counter-offer through the platform.

4. Secure Transaction & Logistics

Once agreed, the platform facilitates secure payment processing and coordinates logistics through partner providers.
